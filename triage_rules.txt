RED (Immediate):
- SpO2 < 90
- Heart rate > 130 AND (chest pain OR breathing difficulty)
- Fainting AND age ≥ 60
- Active bleeding AND heart rate > 120

ORANGE (Very Urgent):
- SpO2 between 90 and 93
- Heart rate ≥ 110
- Temperature ≥ 39 AND breathing difficulty
- Age ≥ 65 AND (chest pain OR breathing difficulty)
- Ambulance arrival AND any symptom present

YELLOW (Can Wait):
- Temperature between 37.5 and 38.9
- Heart rate between 100 and 109
- Chronic disease with stable vitals

GREEN (Low Risk):
- SpO2 ≥ 95
- Heart rate 60–99
- Temperature < 37.5
- No major symptoms
